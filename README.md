# Unicorn Power Collector
### A Magic Game
*Link:* https://embeutler.github.io/UNICORN/

Click the Unicorns (each with hidden numbers) to grow Your Unicorn Powers. To Win the magic match Your Power to the Unicorn Power Goal.
